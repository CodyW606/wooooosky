About Wooooosky

Our Story

Try the signature bourbon of wrestling legend Ric Flair - WOOOOOSKY - a bourbon that embodies the spirit of excellence and showmanship. Coming in 2025, this premium bourbon will redefine what it means to style and profile.

The Spirit

Every bottle of WOOOOOSKY represents the pinnacle of craftsmanship, blending tradition with the unmistakable Flair that only the Nature Boy could bring to bourbon. Carefully aged and meticulously crafted to be as legendary as its namesake.

The Legacy

WOOOOOSKY isn't just another bourbon - it's a celebration of the greatest of all time, a toast to the lifestyle, and a tribute to those who dare to follow in the footsteps of the Greatest of All Time. Join us in 2025 for the most anticipated bourbon launch in history.
